Join the data revolution

Actian is not just a place to work, it’s a place to innovate and our dynamic team thrives on creating technology for the next generation. We are rapidly growing and want you to join us in transforming data management and integration.

Where you are first

“Employee-first” is not just a mantra at Actian, it is the way we do business. We place a huge emphasis on our people and our culture, providing the opportunity for every employee to show up as their authentic self which enables our employees to do their very best work.

Explore Open Positions

An award-winning workplace

Join our dynamic culture and team of enthusiastic, bright minds and be part of an award-winning workplace where you can innovate and collaborate in a context that has a lasting, transformative impact in the worlds of data management, integration, and analytics.

Why Actian?

Our Rewards & Recognition Program, called IMPACTIAN, showcases the wonderful work and contributions each team member brings to Actian! We also offer seasonal events, and weekly gatherings (including virtual) that allow you to meet new colleagues that you might not get a chance to interact with otherwise.

You and your family

We understand the importance of being able to take care of yourself and your loved ones and offer a comprehensive benefits package that includes a variety of health insurance plans, life and disability insurance, retirement plans, a paid parental leave program, and much more.

Time for yourself

Access generous time-off benefits so you can spend time with family, explore new destinations, or have a recharge day. Actian also offers an on-demand resource for employees to explore wellness opportunities for better physical and mental health.

Invest in your future

Bonus or sales variable plans are available to all our employees so they can further invest in their futures.

Growth and opportunity

Regardless of where you are in the world, Actian invests in our workforce! We offer a professional development program so you can further grow your skills and career development. And we firmly believe in great hires through your network and compensate accordingly.

It’s just who we are

At Actian, we believe in culture add, and we don’t subscribe to ideologies that limit people’s potential. We don’t want people to just work here — we want them to belong here.

Rewards & recognition

We want employees to be recognized for their achievements and aligning to Actian’s Core Values. That’s why we have a quarterly peer-peer nomination and an annual team award through our Kudos Award Program.

Corporate social responsibility

Employees are provided three (3) VTO (volunteer time off) days per year to give back to their communities. We also encourage donations and match 1:1 up to $1,000 per calendar year, per employee. Additionally, Actian has made a commitment to donate to our pillars of commitment: STEM, Food Insecurity, and Climate & Sustainability.

DEIB Vision

At Actian, we don’t believe in culture fit. We believe in culture add, and we don’t subscribe to ideologies that limit people’s potential. We evolve because we empower and encourage everyone to be authentic to themselves and accepting of others, creating an open and supportive culture where all voices are valued. This is how we believe true innovation happens. We don’t just want people to work here – we want them to belong here
